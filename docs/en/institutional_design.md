# Institutional Design and Practice

Nagi's institutions begin close to everyday life.

Every decision should have reasons, a point of review, alternative routes, and a place to seek advice. Systems that affect daily life are not entrusted to one AI or one organization alone.

Nagi preserves differences among places and connects them in solidarity only where connection is needed.
