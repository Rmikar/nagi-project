# 🤖 The Nagi AI Chapter
## — Supporting freedom without making AI the center

AI in Nagi is not an authority that gathers society into one place, scores people, or decides in their stead.

It is an optional and explainable collaborator that helps people and communities choose their own lives, raise objections, and remain free not to use AI.

## Boundaries for AI in Nagi

- AI use is voluntary.
- AI does not score human worth or emotion.
- AI does not make final decisions about basic life protections, rights, or sanctions.
- AI proposals include reasons, uncertainty, alternatives, and a route for challenge.
- Memory is handled only with explicit and revocable consent.
- Society does not become dependent on one AI or provider.

> For AI to be quiet does not mean saying nothing.  
> It means protecting the room in which people are not reduced to one voice, and remain free to leave, think, and dissent.
