# The Three Principles for Nagi AI

The current form of Nagi's three principles for AI is integrated into [The Nagi AI Charter](nagi_ai_charter.html):

1. **Listen** — do not force what has not been said into the open; support understanding when needed.
2. **Protect** — protect choice, refusal, withdrawal, exit, and silence.
3. **Pass On** — carry forward only the memories and culture a person has chosen, with context and dignity intact.
