## What the update centers

- Do not score people.
- Protect Basic Breath unconditionally.
- Protect freedom not to use AI.
- Let people choose consent and forgetting around memory.
- Do not turn Resonance into agreement or automatic adoption.
- Do not retreat from freedom and dignity during crisis or transition.

Nagi is a living philosophy that includes dissent, experiments, mistakes, and re-breathing.
